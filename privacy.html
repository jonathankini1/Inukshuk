Privacy Notice

How this website handles photographs, enquiries and basic technical information.

Plain-English summary: photographs used in the land visualizer stay on your device. This website does not use advertising trackers or analytics cookies.

Who this notice covers

This notice applies to visitors using the Inukshuk website at inukshuk-plum.vercel.app. The project team is responsible for information received through project enquiries.

Information you choose to provide

The enquiry form helps you prepare an email containing your name, email address, property location, approximate land size, preferred project scale, timing and project notes. The website does not transmit or store that information by itself; your own email application opens so you can review and send it.

Your land photographs

Photos selected in the “See It on Your Land” tool are processed locally in your browser. They are not uploaded to this website or a third party. A preview is downloaded only when you choose the download button.

Technical information

Like most hosted websites, our hosting provider may process basic request information such as IP address, browser type, requested page and time of access for security, reliability and abuse prevention. Google Fonts may also receive basic request information when the site’s typefaces are loaded.

Cookies and analytics

We do not currently use analytics, advertising cookies or behavioural tracking. If that changes, this notice will be updated and any consent controls required by law will be added.

How enquiry information is used

If you send an enquiry by email, its contents are used to respond, assess project suitability, prepare a proposal and maintain appropriate business records. Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for those purposes or to meet legal obligations.

Your choices and rights

You may ask to access, correct or delete personal information held about you, or object to its use where applicable. Use the website enquiry section to make a privacy request.

Changes to this notice

This notice may be updated as the service develops. The latest version will always appear on this page. Last updated: 19 July 2026.
